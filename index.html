Introduction

PT Seago Logistik Maritim (hereinafter, "SEAGO", "we", "us", or "our") respects your personal privacy and is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, suppliers, ship operators, and partners. This Privacy Policy details our practices concerning the collection, storage, processing, transfer, protection, and disclosure of personal data collected through the SEAGO MARKET mobile applications (available on the Google Play Store and other distribution platforms) and the website https://seago.id (collectively, the "Platform").

By registering an account, utilizing the Platform, or engaging with our maritime marketplace and logistics services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. We comply with all applicable Indonesian regulations concerning personal data protection, including Law No. 27 of 2022 on Personal Data Protection ("UU PDP"), Law No. 11 of 2008 on Electronic Information and Transactions (as amended, "UU ITE"), and Ministry of Communication and Informatics Regulation No. 20 of 2016 on Personal Data Protection in Electronic Systems.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our specialized maritime marketplace, ship supply, and logistics integration services, we collect various categories of information. This information is processed and stored in our database in compliance with Indonesian data protection guidelines (UU PDP).

Information Provided Voluntarily

During user registration, store onboarding, and order checkout processes, we collect the following data:

Customer & User Accounts

Username, Email Address, Phone Number, and cryptographically hashed passwords. Users can be registered under 'user' or 'admin' access roles.

Mitra Toko (Store Partners)

Store Name, business description, store logo, address, phone number, email address, and bank routing credentials (bank name, account holder's name, and bank account number) for processing store payouts.

Transaction, Shipping, and Payment Data

When you place an order or coordinate ship supplies on the Platform, we process details related to transaction fulfillment:

  • Consignee Details: Customer name, telephone contact, and delivery/shipping address (representing the vessel landing docks at the port).
  • Vessel Schedule Details: We collect ship arrival days (vessel ETA schedules) to synchronize time-sensitive maritime logistics deliveries.
  • Transaction Metadata: Total order amount, specific ordering items, quantities, and custom delivery notes.
  • Payment Verification: Selected payment method ('cod' cash on delivery or 'transfer' bank transfer) and uploaded payment proof files (images or receipt files representing bank transfers) for verification.

AI Chat Assistant Interactions

When you converse with our AI Chat Assistant (powered by the Google Gemini API) on the Platform, we process the text prompts and search inquiries you type to retrieve matches from our products directory and offer real-time recommendations.

Information Collected Automatically

When you access our platform via computer or mobile applications, we collect telemetry and device information:

  • Device Metadata: IP Address, device model, Operating System version, session tokens, and web browser type.
  • Platform Activity: Pages visited, clicked links, session timestamps, and product catalog searches.
  • Location Information: Precise geographical location (GPS coords) is collected only if you explicitly grant permission on your mobile device or browser. This is used to coordinate shipping routes and estimated coordinates of maritime vessels for delivery tracking.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process personal data for legal, operational, and development purposes. The processing is conducted under Indonesian data protection regulations (UU PDP) to fulfill our contractual obligations to you and our legal duties. Specifically, we use your data for:

Account & Service Operations

Facilitating customer registration, merchant onboarding, validating store profiles, and authorizing secure user authentication.

Order Fulfillment & Logistics

Routing purchase orders to relevant stores, organizing port drop-offs, synchronizing schedules via ship arrival days, and generating automatic handover documents and invoices (PDF).

Gemini AI Integrations

Powering the AI Chat Assistant to match customer messages with product lists, and parsing merchant invoices for inventory updates through the Gemini AI Bulk Import.

WhatsApp Communication

Forwarding checkout orders and instant notifications directly to Admin and Merchant WhatsApp numbers to speed up response times.

Payment Proof Verification

Reviewing and validating uploaded payment proof bank receipts to transition purchase status from pending to verified.

Security & Error Logs

Monitoring session tokens to prevent hijacking, keeping error logs for system maintenance, and shielding database structures from SQL injections.

3. Data Sharing

PT Seago Logistik Maritim does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

We may share necessary personal data only with trusted third-party service providers and corporate partners under strict data processing agreements that comply with the UU PDP. Data is shared with:

  1. Shipping Companies & Suppliers: To coordinate orders, ship supply provisioning, and logistics drop-offs at designated ports.
  2. Logistics Partners: Freight operators, maritime agents, and delivery personnel servicing the ports.
  3. Payment Gateways & Banks: Banking institutions and payment processors to verify and reconcile bank transfer deposits.
  4. Cloud Infrastructure Providers: Secure hosting environments, database engines, and backup systems managing the digital infrastructure in compliance with Indonesian data residency standards.
  5. Google Gemini API: Chat prompts and queries inputted inside the AI Chat Assistant (`ai_chat.php`) are forwarded to Google Gemini APIs to fetch matched products and prices dynamically.
  6. WhatsApp Services: Order invoices and customer telephone coordinates are forwarded via WhatsApp to store owners and administrators to execute procurement updates.
  7. Legal & Regulatory Authorities: Competent government bodies, courts, or law enforcement officers when strictly required by Indonesian legislation or judicial orders.

4. Data Security

We implement a robust, defense-in-depth security framework to safeguard personal data against unauthorized access, manipulation, leakage, or loss. Our administrative and technical protections include:

Network Encryption

Mandatory HTTPS connection protocol across all services with transport-layer SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit.

Access Controls

Strict role-based access control (RBAC) ensuring only authorized SEAGO compliance staff can interact with core user data.

Cryptography

Standardized password hashing (salted bcrypt/argon2) preventing credential exposures and secure database encryption at rest.

Infrastructure Defense

Enterprise firewalls, security incident monitoring (SIEM), routine vulnerability assessments, and automatic offsite backups.

5. User Rights

In accordance with Chapter VI of Law No. 27 of 2022 on Personal Data Protection (UU PDP), as a data subject, you hold specific enforceable statutory rights. You may execute these rights at any time by contacting our support team:

  • Right to Access: Request a comprehensive copy of your personal data stored in our databases.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated data associated with your SEAGO MARKET account.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): Request the permanent deletion of your account and related records, subject to regulatory retention laws.
  • Right to Data Portability: Obtain a structured, commonly used machine-readable copy of your personal data for transmission to another data controller.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent previously granted for optional processing (e.g., location tracking, marketing alerts).
  • Right to Object: Oppose or request restriction of data processing for specific activities.

6. Cookie Usage

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (such as local web storage and beacons) to enhance user navigation and maintain state parameters. You can adjust your browser settings to reject cookies, though some platform features may cease to function correctly.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Authentication & Session Cookies: Essential for keeping you logged in as you navigate the maritime marketplace.
  • Shopping Cart & Preferences: Remember items added to purchase orders and retain selected filters or UI preferences.
  • Analytics & Performance: Aggregated, anonymous metrics (via Google Analytics) to study user counts, layout efficacy, and page speeds.

7. Third Party Services

The SEAGO MARKET mobile applications and website use several external services and libraries to provide core marketplace, payment, and AI operations. These third-party services process data under their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Gemini API (Generative Language Service): Used to power the AI Chat Assistant and the administrator's bulk product scanner. Product catalogs, chat prompts, and uploaded bills/nota data are parsed using Google Gemini AI.
  • WhatsApp Business / Link Integration: Used to route checkout orders, receipt confirmations, and merchant notification alerts between crews, store owners, and administrators.
  • Google Play Services: Core application utilities and framework integration for Android devices.
  • Firebase: Secure database synchronization, real-time analytics, push notification routing, and application crash telemetry (Crashlytics).
  • Google Analytics: Aggregated analytical tracking of Platform traffic, active users, and system performance metrics.
  • Maps & Geolocation APIs: Used to display vessel schedules and coordinate freight routing at the ports of Ambon, Maluku.
  • Payment Gateways & Banking Systems: Verified payment systems processing bank transfers and QRIS transactions.

8. Children's Privacy

Our platform, SEAGO MARKET, operates as a business-to-business (B2B) maritime logistics and ship supply procurement platform. Our services are intended exclusively for business representatives, shipping entities, and individuals who are at least 18 years old or legally competent under Indonesian Civil Law. We do not knowingly collect or request personal data from individuals under 18 years of age. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under 18 without parental consent, we will delete that information immediately.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as your account remains active or as required to fulfill the business purposes detailed in this policy. When data is no longer necessary for these purposes, we safely delete or anonymize it.

Under Indonesian financial, accounting, and tax laws, we are legally required to retain transaction histories, invoicing records, and corporate tax receipts for a minimum period of 5 (five) years. Data may also be retained for longer periods if required under active court proceedings or statutory litigation periods.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may modify or revise this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our services, technological advancements, or changes in Indonesian personal data protection laws (UU PDP). If we make material modifications, we will notify you through prominent alerts on our website homepage, in-app notifications, or via email before the changes become active. The "Last Updated" timestamp at the top of this page will change accordingly. We encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed about our data protection practices.
